14. A business uncovers cash revenue that is split into small deposits across multiple bank branches to avoid triggering automated reporting limits. What stage of money laundering does this reflect?
Solution Note: Money laundering follows three stages: Placement (introducing dirty cash into the financial system), Layering (moving funds through complex links to hide the trail), and Integration (reintroducing the clean cash into the economy). Splitting deposits is a Placement technique.
