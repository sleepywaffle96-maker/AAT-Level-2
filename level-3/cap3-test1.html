14. Which inventory pricing methodology can be used for internal management costing purposes but is explicitly banned by IAS 2 for external financial reporting?
Solution Note: IAS 2 explicitly forbids LIFO because it results in closing inventory valuations that are locked to older historic costs, heavily understating modern current asset values on the Statement of Financial Position.
