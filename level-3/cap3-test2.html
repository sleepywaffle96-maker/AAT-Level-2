Solution Note: Under-absorption means product unit costs were underestimated during the period. The shortfall must be debited to the SPL as an additional overhead expense, reducing net profit.

6. When performing overhead apportionment under a traditional 'Step-Down' allocation model, how are service cost centers managed if they provide support to each other?
Solution Note: Step-down allocation establishes a sequence. The service department that provides services to the greatest number of other service departments is closed first. Costs cannot flow back to a department that has already been cleared to zero.

Solution Note: IAS 2 bans LIFO because the remaining closing stock would reflect older purchase prices, leaving current assets on the balance sheet detached from modern market values.
