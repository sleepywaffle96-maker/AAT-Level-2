12. An old trade client whose invoice was completely written off as irrecoverable during a prior financial exercise suddenly pays the company £500 cash. What is the correct entry?
Solution Note: Because the historical customer subsidiary account was terminated in a prior year, the recovery cash receipt cannot touch current receivables pools. It is treated as independent operating revenue via the Irrecoverable Debts Recovered account.

14. Why does the general allowance mechanism apply a risk percentage to potential defaults while ignoring potential unexpected windfall revenues?
Solution Note: The principle of Prudence mandates asymmetrical caution: recognize liabilities and losses as soon as they are realistically anticipated, but never anticipate revenues.
