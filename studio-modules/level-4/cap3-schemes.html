2. Comprehensive Planning & Operational Variance Deconstruction Map

Visual representation of variance splitting. This model isolates non-controllable budget planning errors from controllable operational execution.

[ Original Standard Budget ] ──► e.g., Material Standard set at £10.00/Kg
              │
              ├─► PLANNING VARIANCE (Adverse £1.50/Kg)
              │   Market price surged nationwide due to new trade tariffs.
              ▼
[ Revised Realistic Standard ] ──► Recalculated at £11.50/Kg (Non-controllable baseline)
              │
              ├─► OPERATIONAL VARIANCE (Favourable £0.50/Kg)
              │   Local Procurement Manager negotiated a bulk contract at £11.00/Kg.
              ▼
[ Actual Results Achieved ] ───► Finished production at £11.00/Kg actual cost.

Analytical Insight: A traditional audit would show an absolute variance of £1.00 Adverse (£10.00 standard vs £11.00 actual), penalizing the purchasing team. Deconstruction reveals that the manager performed efficiently, saving £0.50 per Kg against realistic market conditions.
