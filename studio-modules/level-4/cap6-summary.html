DAIF Formula Cheat Sheet & Reference

Essential core mathematical equations for interpretation and financial analysis exams.

1. Profitability and Du Pont Component Ratios

Return on Capital Employed (ROCE %)
ROCE = (Operating Profit [PBIT] / [Total Equity + Non-Current Liabilities]) x 100
Asset Turnover Ratio
Asset Turnover = Revenue / (Total Equity + Non-Current Liabilities) [expressed as times]

2. Liquidity & Working Capital Timeline Formulations

Acid Test (Quick Ratio)
Acid Test = (Total Current Assets - Inventory) / Total Current Liabilities
Receivables Collection Period
Receivables Days = (Average Trade Receivables / Credit Sales Revenue) x 365

3. Capital Structure & Leverage Vulnerabilities

Capital Gearing Ratio %
Gearing = (Long-Term Non-Current Liabilities / [Total Equity + Long-Term Liabilities]) x 100
Interest Cover Ratio
Interest Cover = Operating Profit [PBIT] / Annual Finance Costs [expressed as times]

4. Ratio Interpretation Rules Reference

  • Overtrading Symptoms: Plunging liquidity metrics matching high revenue growth, dropping bank balances, and surging trade payables days.
  • Window Dressing: Executing short-term transactions right before the year-end cutoff to temporarily distort accounting benchmarks.
  • Gearing Alert: Structures exceeding 50% gearing require a high interest cover ratio (>3.0 times) to guarantee solvency during economic downturns.
