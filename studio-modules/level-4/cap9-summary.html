3. Critical Regulatory Terms Quick Guide

  • Public Interest: The collective well-being of the community and institutions that professional accountants are legally and ethically bound to safeguard.
  • Greenwashing: Disclosing misleading or exaggerated eco-friendly statements to mask poor environmental management.
  • NOCLAR (Non-Compliance with Laws and Regulations): The international standard setting out an accountant's duty to report client operational illegalities to external authorities.

4. Corporate Anti-Malpractice Boundaries

  • Criminal Offence Surcharges: Under POCA 2002, failing to disclose money laundering or tipping off a suspect carries immediate prison liabilities.
  • Safeguard Execution: If an accountant cannot implement practical safeguards to reduce an ethical threat to an acceptable level, they must refuse the task or resign.
