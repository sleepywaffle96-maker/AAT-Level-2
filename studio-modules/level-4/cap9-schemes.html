2. Advanced Ethical Conflict Resolution Pathway

The official systematic matrix mapping actions required when dealing with material financial malpractice.

Conflict Resolution Stage Action Protocol Guidelines Risk Mitigation Target
1. Internal Escalation Document facts clearly; report issue to senior leadership or the audit committee. Resolves systemic errors internally without breaching contract confidentiality boundaries.
2. Independent Advice Consult AAT's ethical advisory service under legal privilege parameters. Clarifies statutory disclosure rules before engaging external enforcement agencies.
3. External Disclosure File a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) with the National Crime Agency (NCA) if money laundering is found. Fulfills UK legal anti-money laundering obligations while preventing tipping-off exposures.
