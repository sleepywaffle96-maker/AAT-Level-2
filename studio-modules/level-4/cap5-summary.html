DAIF Group Consolidation Cheat Sheet

Essential mathematical workflows and adjustment rules for corporate group statements.

1. Provision for Unrealised Profit (PUP Formulas)

PUP Calculation via Profit Mark-up %
PUP = Cost Value of Remaining Stock x (Mark-up% / 100)
PUP Calculation via Gross Profit Margin %
PUP = Invoiced Value of Remaining Stock x (Margin% / 100)

2. Statutory Corporate Goodwill Balance Equation (IFRS 3)

Goodwill = Parent Purchase Consideration + Fair Value of NCI - Subsidiary Net Assets at Acquisition

3. Closing Non-Controlling Interest (Working 4)

Closing NCI = NCI Value at Acquisition + (NCI Ownership % x Subsidiary Post-Acquisition Profit)

4. Core Group Consolidation Accounting Rules

  • Line-by-Line Aggregation: Combine 100% of all subsidiary asset and liability lines with the parent's balances. Do not scale them down by the ownership percentage, as the NCI account balances the statement.
  • Pre-Acquisition Profits: The retained earnings of a subsidiary up to the date of its purchase. These are frozen inside the goodwill calculation and can never be used to distribute dividends.
  • Intra-group Elimination: Cancel out all internal loan, receivable, and payable balances. They must never appear inside consolidated current assets or current liabilities.
  • Investment in Associate (IAS 28): Accounted for using a single line in non-current assets: original purchase cost plus the group's share of post-acquisition retained profits.
