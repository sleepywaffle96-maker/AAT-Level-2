AMAC Budgeting Cheat Sheet & Equations

Essential mathematical forecasting algorithms and strategic rules for rapid exam recall.

1. Time Series Forecasting Equations (Linear Trend Modeling)

The Linear Regression Formula
y = a + bx

y = Total forecast output value | a = Fixed baseline intercept | b = Linear trend gradient multiplier | x = Specific future time period point

2. Consolidated Master Budget Balancing Algorithms

Production Volume Formula (Units)
Production Required = Forecast Sales Units + Target Closing Stock Cushion - Opening Inventory b/f
Material Purchase Budget Formula (£)
Purchases Needed = [(Production Units x Unit Component Spec) + Target Closing Material - Opening Material] x Standard Price per Kg

3. Multiplicative Seasonal Adjustment Check

Actual Forecasted Value (Adjusted) = Trend Line Projection (y) x Seasonal Factor Fraction

4. Strategic Budgeting Control Checklist

  • Principal Budget Factor: The initial corporate bottleneck constraint (typically sales demand) that establishes the construction order of subsequent schedules.
  • Budgetary Slack: Deliberate inflation of costs or deflation of sales targets by operational managers to secure easy performance evaluations.
  • ZBB Decision Package: An operational micro-document profiling a distinct business activity, analyzing alternate execution models, and evaluating its cost-benefit ratio from a zero baseline.
  • Rolling Buffer: Replacing static assumptions by dynamically appending a future calendar block as the earliest month or quarter expires, maintaining a constant forward-looking horizon.
