AMAC Advanced Costing Calculations

Core mathematical formulas required for Activity-Based, Target, and Throughput exams.

1. Activity-Based Costing Rates Formula

Cost Driver Rate (CDR)
CDR = Total Financial Cost of Pool / Total Volume of Selected Cost Driver

2. Throughput Metric Equations

Throughput Contribution Per Unit
Throughput = Selling Price - Direct Material Cost ONLY
Throughput Accounting Ratio (TA Ratio)
TA Ratio = (Throughput Per Bottleneck Hour) / (Factory Cost Per Bottleneck Hour)

A product is financially viable only if the TA Ratio is greater than 1.0.

3. Target Metric Equations

Target Cost = Competitive Market Price - Required Strategic Profit Margin

4. Strategic Costing Cheat Sheet

  • The Bottleneck Resource: The absolute limiting resource or machine that acts as the constraint hindering structural output speed.
  • Factory Costs: Include all operating costs (Direct labor, overheads, rent, utilities) except direct raw materials.
  • Value Engineering: Systematic analysis aimed at reducing a component's structural cost gap without reducing overall quality.
