2. Net Operating Cycle Days & Liquidity Asset Map

The mathematical pipeline mapping day limits for processing physical items back into monetary value.

Working Capital Cycle Phase Standard Formula Model Operational Goal Direction Cash Flow Implications
1. Inventory Holding Period (Average Inventory / Cost of Sales) x 365 Minimize (e.g., target 45 days) Prevents cash from being tied up in raw materials and finished stock.
2. Trade Receivables Period (Average Receivables / Credit Sales) x 365 Minimize (e.g., target 30 days) Speeds up the conversion of credit invoices into raw bank deposits.
3. Less: Trade Payables Period (Average Payables / Credit Purchases) x 365 Maximize (e.g., target 60 days) Delays outward payments to retain liquid cash inside the company bank account.
NET OPERATING CYCLE TOTAL Days (1 + 2) - Days 3 Target: Lean Cycle Profile Measures the net number of days cash is tied up in trading loops.
