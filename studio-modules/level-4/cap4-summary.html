DAIF Regulatory Cheat Sheet

Essential formulas and classification boundaries for accounting standard compliance checks.

1. Inventory Valuation Calculation Constraints (IAS 2)

Net Realisable Value (NRV) Equation
NRV = Projected Selling Price - Estimated Completion Costs - Estimated Selling Expenses

2. IAS 16 Non-Current Asset Revaluation Elements

Revaluation Surplus Calculation
Surplus = Regular Fair Value Valuation - Net Book Value at Date of Revaluation

3. IAS 36 Impairment Metric Boundaries

Recoverable Amount Condition
Recoverable Amount = HIGHER of (Fair Value Less Costs to Sell) and (Value in Use)

An asset is impaired if its carrying amount on the SoFP exceeds this Recoverable Amount.

4. Corporate Reporting Core Rules

  • Going Concern: The baseline structural assumption that the company will continue trading and operating for at least 12 months from the reporting date.
  • Adjusting Event: Post-reporting-date evidence confirming financial conditions that were already present at year-end (e.g., bankruptcy of a credit customer).
  • Constructive Obligation: Arises when previous company practices or published policies create a valid corporate expectation that the firm will accept a liability.
