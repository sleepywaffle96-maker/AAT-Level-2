DAIF Standard Group Consolidation Workings

The essential step-by-step mathematical working sheets required to draft consolidated statements under IFRS rules.

1. Working 2: Full Goodwill Valuation Schedule (IFRS 3)

The standardized structure used to calculate goodwill at the acquisition date, including fair value adjustments.

Goodwill Component Breakdown Parent Share (£) Consolidated Pool Total (£)
Purchase Consideration Transferred by Parent Company 520,000
Add: Fair Value of the Non-Controlling Interest (NCI) at Acquisition 140,000
Total Combined Value Pool 660,000
Less: Subsidiary Net Identifiable Assets at Acquisition:
- Ordinary Share Capital Baseline Value (250,000)
- Retained Earnings at Acquisition Date Milestone (190,000)
- Land Fair Value Revaluation Surplus Adjustments (40,000)
FINAL ASSET GOODWILL ARISING (Carried to SoFP Assets) 180,000

2. Working 5: Group Retained Earnings Calculation

Advanced working model to calculate consolidated retained profits, accounting for post-acquisition shares and PUP adjustments.

Parent Company Retained Earnings Balance (100% Core) .........     £920,000
Add: Group Share of Subsidiary Post-Acquisition Profit:
     Subsidiary Retained Earnings at Reporting Date . £265,000
     Less: Subsidiary Retained Earnings at Acquisition -£190,000
     Total Subsidiary Post-Acquisition Profit Pool ..  £75,000
     Group Allocation Share (80% Parent Share) ..............    + £60,000
Less: Provision for Unrealised Profit (PUP: Parent Selling) ..    -  £4,200
Less: Accumulated Goodwill Impairment Surcharges (Group Share)    -  £6,000
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CONSOLIDATED RETAINED EARNINGS BALANCE (Carried to Equity) ...    £969,800

Consolidation Rule Reminder: If the Subsidiary was the selling entity in the intra-group transaction, the PUP adjustment must be deducted from Working 3 (Subsidiary Net Assets), reducing both the Parent's and the NCI's shares proportionately rather than impacting Working 5 directly.
