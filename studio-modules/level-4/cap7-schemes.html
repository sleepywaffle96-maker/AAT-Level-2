1. System Deficiency Assessment Matrix

The standard reporting structure used to map control gaps, business risks, and solutions.

Identified Control Deficiency Associated Corporate Risk Recommended System Control
Accounting staff share a single master database log-in credential. Total loss of individual audit trails; adjustments cannot be traced to specific users. Enforce unique individual user log-ins with mandatory monthly password resets.
Purchase ledger accounts are reconciled only once every six months. Delayed identification of processing errors, supplier payment issues, or duplicate billing. Mandate monthly supplier statement reconciliations signed off by an accounting supervisor.
Physical inventory counts are overseen by warehouse managers. Lack of independence; risk of staff hiding stock shrinkage, damages, or theft. Appoint independent inventory count teams not connected to warehouse staff.
