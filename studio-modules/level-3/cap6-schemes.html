BUAW Internal Control Weaknesses & Resolution Flow

Visual analysis grids mapping accounting control deficiencies, potential business risks, and standard ethical escalation pathways.

1. System Deficiency Assessment Matrix

The standard audit template used to identify transaction flaws and design effective systems control check gates.

Identified Accounting Flaw Associated Risk / Vulnerability Recommended Control Gating Update
Purchase invoices are processed and paid without verifying delivery documents. Paying for duplicate supplier bills, incorrect quantities, or goods that were never received. Enforce a three-way matching control: Purchase Invoice must align with the Purchase Order and Goods Received Note (GRN) before payment approval.
The petty cash box is kept in an unlocked drawer with multiple employees accessing the change. Theft of cash, missing transaction logs, and an inability to trace ledger adjustments to specific staff. Keep petty cash secured inside a locked safe. Assign a single custodian and mandate signed receipts for every withdrawal.
The database profile used to modify employee payroll parameters requires no secondary manager sign-off. Creation of ghost employees, falsified overtime submissions, or unauthorized wage increases. Configure a dual-authorization check gate: payroll adjustments must be signed off by a senior manager before the system updates the bank file.
