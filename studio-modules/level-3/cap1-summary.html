The Opening Reversals Law (1st January Postings)

At the start of a new financial exercise (1st January), the closing balances of Accruals and Prepayments recorded on 31st December must be completely reversed into their respective expense accounts. This Opening Reversal Entry wipes out the temporary financial statement balances and guarantees that subsequent cash entries from bank statements are automatically reduced by the amount already accounted for, preventing catastrophic cost duplication errors.
