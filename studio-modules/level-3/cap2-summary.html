🎯 CORE FINANCIAL ADJUSTMENT EQUATIONS:

1. Operating Overhead SPL Charge = Bank Account Total Paid + Closing Accrual - Closing Prepayment
2. Net Adjusted Receivables Pool = Pre-Adjustment Control Balance - Specific Bad Debt Write-offs
3. Required Allowance Target Pool = Net Adjusted Receivables Pool × General Provision Risk Rate (%)
4. Reducing Balance Depreciation = (Historic Capital Cost - Accumulated Depreciation Pool) × Depreciation Rate (%)

🎯 STRATEGIC FINAL ACCOUNTS PREPARATION CHECKLIST:
  • The Reversal Law Mandate: Double-check that opening ledger adjustments for Accruals and Prepayments b/f from the prior exercise are strictly reversed on the first day of the new period to eliminate cost duplication traps.
  • The Specific Bad Debt Exclusion: Never compute the general allowance for doubtful debts percentage on the unadjusted receivables balance. Always perform specific customer write-offs first, then apply the rate to the clean pool balance.
  • Capitalization Attributable Law: Verify that delivery charges, professional installation fees, or legal acquisition taxes are fully capitalized into the non-current asset cost account under IAS 16 guidelines, bypassing the operating expense streams.
