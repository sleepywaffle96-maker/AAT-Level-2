FAPS Cheat Sheet & Formulas

Essential formulas, margins, mark-ups, and structural concepts for exam revision.

1. Mark-up vs Margin Formulas

Mark-up (Profit based on Cost Price)
Profit / Cost Price x 100

Cost is always 100%. Selling Price = 100% + Mark-up%

Margin (Profit based on Selling Price)
Profit / Selling Price x 100

Revenue/Selling Price is always 100%. Cost = 100% - Margin%

2. The Accounting Equation (Extended)

Assets = Liabilities + Opening Capital + Profit - Drawings

3. Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) Framework

COGS = Opening Inventory + Purchases + Carriage Inwards - Closing Inventory

4. Key Exam Rules Quick Guide

  • Carriage Inwards: Added to purchases in COGS calculation (Capitalised inside trading account).
  • Carriage Outwards: Distribution cost, expensed directly to the administrative section of the SoPL.
  • Irrecoverable debt recovery: Credit the SoPL as separate miscellaneous income or reduce the irrecoverable debt expense line.
  • Partnership Act 1890: In the absence of an agreement, profits are shared equally, no salaries are paid, and no interest on capital is allowed.
