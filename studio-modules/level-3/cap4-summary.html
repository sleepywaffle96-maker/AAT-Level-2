TPFB Cheat Sheet & Tax Formulas

Essential parameters and parsing methods for extraction tasks within UK tax computations.

1. VAT Extraction Formulas (Standard Rate 20%)

Extracting VAT from a Gross Total
VAT Amount = Gross Value x (20 / 120) [or Gross / 6]
Calculating Net from a Gross Total
Net Value = Gross Value / 1.20

2. Fuel Scale Charges

If a business reclaims 100% of input VAT on fuel for a car used both commercially and privately, it must add back a fixed statutory output tax amount called the Fuel Scale Charge to account for private usage.

3. Disallowed Input VAT Rules (Exclusions)

  • Business Entertainment: VAT cannot be reclaimed on entertaining UK clients (except staff events).
  • Passenger Motor Cars: 100% blocked on new cars unless used entirely for business (e.g., taxi, driving school). Lease cars usually have a 50% recovery block.

4. Fuel & Travel Special Rules

  • Public Transport: Train, bus, and flight bookings inside the UK are zero-rated (0%).
  • Petrol/Diesel: Input VAT recovery requires valid VAT receipts containing HMRC-compliant tax breakdown figures.
