BUAW Cheat Sheet & Ethics Core

Core definitions and threat classification rules for rapid exam recall.

1. Core Threats to Ethical Principles

Self-Interest Threat

Financial interests, shares, or potential job offers affecting judgment.

Self-Review Threat

Auditing or checking financial statements that you prepared yourself.

Familiarity Threat

Being too sympathetic or close to a client due to a long-standing personal relationship.

Advocacy Threat

Promoting a client’s position or shares to the point that objectivity is compromised.

Intimidation Threat

Being deterred from acting objectively by physical or professional threats/dismissal pressures.

2. Key Legal Terms Quick Guide

  • Tipping Off: Telling someone they are suspected of money laundering (Criminal Offence under POCA 2002).
  • Whistleblowing: Exposing illegal activities or fraud within an organisation to an external regulator.
  • Three-Way Match: Corroborating a Purchase Invoice against its Purchase Order and Goods Received Note (GRN).
  • Principles-Based Framework: Focuses on the core spirit of ethical rules, preventing accountants from finding technical loopholes.
