Chapter 3.1 Schemes: Visual Ledger Flowcharts & Three-Column Cash Book Mappings

Questo schema mappa visivamente l'instradamento geometrico delle transazioni liquide dal Three-Column Cash Book verso i rispettivi Ledger sussidiari e generali.

📊 FLOW 1: THE THREE-COLUMN GEOMETRY & LEDGER ROUTING
[ SIDE ] [ COLUMN ] [ DOUBLE ENTRY / LEDGER ROUTING INTERACTION ] ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- DEBIT Cash Column -----> DEBIT Entry: Physical cash receipts coming in. (Receipts) Bank Column -----> DEBIT Entry: Bank deposits, electronic receipts (BACS/Debits). Discount Allowed --> MEMORANDUM ONLY: Totals debited to Discount Allowed A/c (General Ledger). ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- CREDIT Cash Column -----> CREDIT Entry: Physical cash expenditure leaving safe. (Payments) Bank Column -----> CREDIT Entry: Bank transfers, cheques drawn, direct debits out. Discount Received -> MEMORANDUM ONLY: Totals credited to Discount Received A/c (General Ledger).
🔄 FLOW 2: CONTRA ENTRIES MECCANICA (Codice 'C' in Folio)

Una Contra Entry si verifica quando il denaro viene spostato internamente tra il conto Cassa Fisico e il Conto Corrente Bancario, senza modificare il patrimonio aziendale. La partita doppia si chiude interamente dentro il Cash Book.

A. Versamento contanti in Banca:
  • DEBIT: Bank Column (Aumento banca)
  • CREDIT: Cash Column (Diminuzione cassa)
  • Folio Reference: Inserire 'C' in Dare e Avere.
B. Prelievo banca per cassa centrale:
  • DEBIT: Cash Column (Aumento cassa)
  • CREDIT: Bank Column (Diminuzione banca)
  • Folio Reference: Inserire 'C' in Dare e Avere.
⏱️ FLOW 3: THE IMPREST SYSTEM PIPELINE (MATHEMATICAL CYCLE)

Questo diagramma strutturale mostra la scomposizione geometrica dei voucher e il calcolo del Top-up monetario per ripristinare il fondo iniziale.

[ STEP 1: INITIAL STATE ] Fixed Float Assigned (e.g., £250.00) ---> Stays as Opening Debit asset balance. [ STEP 2: OPERATIONAL PERIOD - EXPENSES RECORDING ] Voucher 01 (Stationery): £30.00 Gross ===> Net Column: £25.00 | VAT Column: £5.00 Voucher 02 (Taxi Fare): £18.00 Gross ===> Net Column: £18.00 | VAT Column: £0.00 (Zero-Rated) Voucher 03 (Cleaning): £24.00 Gross ===> Net Column: £20.00 | VAT Column: #4.00 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ TOTAL EXPENDITURE: £72.00 LORDO ====> Net Totals: £63.00 | VAT Total: £9.00 [ STEP 3: RECONCILIATION EQUATION ] Formula: Cash Remaining in Tin (£178.00) + Total Gross Vouchers (£72.00) = Fixed Float (£250.00) [ STEP 4: BANK REIMBURSEMENT (TOP-UP) ENTRY ] Amount to Draw from Main Bank = Exact Total Gross of Vouchers Spent = £72.00 DOUBLE ENTRY LOGIC FOR THE TOP-UP CHEQUE: • DEBIT : Petty Cash Ledger Account (£72.00) • CREDIT : Main Bank Ledger Account (£72.00)
📑 FLOW 4: END-OF-MONTH DOUBLE ENTRY TRANSFER MATRIX

Al termine del periodo contabile, i totali delle colonne d'analisi del Petty Cash Book vengono trasferiti nel General Ledger tramite partita doppia formale.

FORMAL POSTING JOURNAL:

• DEBIT : Stationery Expense Account (Total Net) -> £25.00
• DEBIT : Travel/Taxi Expense Account (Total Net) -> £18.00
• DEBIT : Cleaning Expense Account (Total Net) -> £20.00
• DEBIT : VAT Control Account (Total VAT Column) -> £9.00

• CREDIT : Petty Cash Control Account (Total Cash Paid) -> £72.00

*Nota: Questa partita doppia di fine mese bilancia perfettamente l'estratto conto della cassa sussidiaria.
