📉 TRADE RECEIVABLES & ALLOWANCE ADJUSTMENT CASCADING FLOW
[ PHASE 1: WRITE OFF SPECIFIC IRRECOVERABLE DEBT ] • DEBIT : Irrecoverable Debts Account (Expense ↑) £500 • CREDIT : Trade Receivables Control Account / SLCA (Asset ↓) £500 [ PHASE 2: NET POOL RECALCULATION ] Formula: Closing Trade Receivables (£42,500) - Irrecoverable Debt written off (£500) = Net Pool £42,000 *Exam Rule: Never apply the allowance percentage on the raw opening receivables balance! [ PHASE 3: EVALUATE ALLOWANCE ADJUSTMENT (Differential Flow) ] Calculate Required Allowance: Net Pool (£42,000) × New Rate (5%) = £2,100 Compare with Opening Balance (£1,800) ===> Variance = £300 INCREASE • DEBIT : Irrecoverable Debts Account (Expense ↑) £300 • CREDIT : Allowance for Doubtful Debts Account (Contro-Asset ↑) £300
